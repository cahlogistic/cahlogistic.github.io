About

Ca.h is a fintech company that want to offer health coaching and a personal health assistant to increase your healthcare by prevention. Our vision is to improve the quality of life through prevention and coaching starting from financial health up to the need for free time, naturally considering mental and physical health. All of that supported by our fintech service.

It is the health specialist who comes to you and no longer vice versa.

Our.Services

Physical health

A team of nurse, supported from our partners, on your service h24 to increase your prevention and decrease your unnecessary visits.

Financial health

Learn how to manage your money is part of your better health. Our purpose is to increase healthcare research and help people to have access to high quality treatments.

Mental health

Lots of time mental health is undervaluated, also because is thought that money and physical health are priorities.

Free Time necessity

Know how to organise and take your free time, for us or for someone we love sometimes is not simple, and that affects our health too.

Healthcare specialists

Preventive Healthcare: What means?

Preventive healthcare deals with the prevention of illness to decrease the burden of disease and associated risk factors. Follow our advice, we will check you remotely, we will send you some survey or directly our nurse to your home.
